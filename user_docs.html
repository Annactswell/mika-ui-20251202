01 Generating Art

The Create Page is your main workspace.

  1. Type your idea in the big yellow box.

    Example: "A cyberpunk cat eating noodles in neon rain"

  2. Select an Aspect Ratio (Square, Landscape, Portrait).
  3. Hit GENERATE and wait for the magic.

02 Prompting 101

Writing good prompts is an art. Here is the Mika formula:

[Subject] + [Action] + [Style] + [Lighting/Mood]
Do

"A golden retriever astronaut, realistic style, dramatic lighting, 8k"

Don't

"Dog space"

04 Credits & Plans

Mika runs on a credit system. Every image generation costs 1 Credit.

  • Free users get 10 credits / day.
  • Pro users get unlimited slow generations + 1000 fast credits.
  • You can buy top-ups in the Pricing Page.
